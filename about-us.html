Who we are

That Will Move You to The Top

Mokka Studios S.r.l. is an innovative startup that produces complex computer systems. We are a multicultural team of creative and talented professionals who understand the importance of high-quality work. Our expertise varies throughout the spheres of e-learning, e-commerce, healthcare, advertising, augmented reality, sharing economy, finance, and more. Bringing together industry professionals and young talents, we are united by a collective vision: delivering digital experiences with a human touch. Our mission is developing complex, innovative computer products to be presented on the market, as well as the production of software for companies, organizations, and institutions.

We are trusted advisors to our clients as we always keep their best interests in mind and quickly respond to their ever-coming needs. We believe that well-crafted software is a product of honest communication and mutual commitment. Our goal is to produce innovative technologies on a human scale accessible to all from an economical and user experience point of view. We want to stimulate the full realization of digital humanism. To further support our vision, we invest in ongoing research and knowledge sharing both to expand our capabilities and give back to the community.
